# RCF - Airbnb Guest Interactions (2023) - Fire & Emergency

> Highest-stakes category in the chatbot. Hallucinations here are unacceptable. Every answer routes to the local emergency number (997) and gives one set of unambiguous instructions.

---

## Context Relevancy: Emergency - kitchen fire

**Project fit:** Guest reporting an active fire in the kitchen.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> 1. Evacuate the apartment and call **997** for firefighter assistance.
> 2. Remain outside in a safe place until the firefighters arrive.

---

## Context Relevancy: Emergency - smoke from electrical outlet

**Project fit:** Guest reporting smoke or sparks from an outlet or appliance.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> 1. Evacuate the apartment and call **997** for firefighter assistance.
> 2. Remain outside in a safe place until the firefighters arrive.

---

## Context Relevancy: Emergency - chemical spill

**Project fit:** Guest reporting a spilled cleaning agent or unknown chemical.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> 1. Evacuate the apartment and get to fresh air.
> 2. Call **997** for firefighter assistance.
> 3. Remain outside in a safe place until the firefighters arrive.

---

## Context Relevancy: Emergency - trapped by smoke in hallway

**Project fit:** Guest reporting they cannot exit because of smoke or fire blocking their route.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> 1. Stay in your apartment and seal the gaps around doors with towels or cloth to prevent smoke from entering.
> 2. Call **997** for firefighter assistance to report your location and situation.
> 3. Signal from a window if possible to alert firefighters to your presence.
