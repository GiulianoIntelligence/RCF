# RCF - Airbnb Guest Interactions (2023) - Check-out

> Common, simple, and easy to hallucinate (the model would otherwise invent times). Pinning the Value kills the variance.

---

## Context Relevancy: Check-out - time

**Project fit:** Guest asking what time they need to be out by.

**Value:**

> Check-out time is at **11:00 AM**.

---

## Context Relevancy: Check-out - procedure

**Project fit:** Guest asking how to formally check out (key drop, app action, etc.).

**Value:**

> You are checked out automatically at 11:00 AM. Please leave the keys on the kitchen counter and ensure the door is closed behind you. No further action is required from your side.

---

## Context Relevancy: Check-out - late check-out request

**Project fit:** Guest asking whether they can stay past the standard time.

**Value:**

> Late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability. Please inquire at least **24 hours** before your scheduled check-out time.
