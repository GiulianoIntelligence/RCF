# RCF - Airbnb Guest Interactions (2023) - Medical

> Every entry routes the guest to **998** (UAE ambulance) and gives one minimal first-aid action while help is en route. No diagnosis, no improvisation.

---

## Context Relevancy: Medical - heavy bleeding from cut

**Project fit:** Guest reporting an active wound with significant blood loss.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> - Call **998** for immediate medical assistance.
> - Apply pressure to the wound with a clean cloth or bandage.

---

## Context Relevancy: Medical - child swallowed a foreign object

**Project fit:** Caregiver reporting that a child has ingested a non-food item.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> - Call **998** for immediate medical assistance.
> - Keep your child calm and monitor their condition.

## Context Relevancy: Medical - chest pain or difficulty breathing

## Context Relevancy: Medical - fall with possible fracture

**Project fit:** Bystander reporting someone fell and cannot move a limb.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> - Call **998** for immediate medical assistance.
> - Keep the injured person still and avoid moving the affected limb.

---

## Context Relevancy: Medical - snake bite

**Project fit:** Guest or bystander reporting a snake bite.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps immediately:
> - Call **998** for immediate medical assistance.
> - Try to keep the bitten person as still as possible to prevent the venom from spreading.
> - Do not cut the wound or try to suck out the venom; these actions can worsen the situation.
> - If possible, keep the bitten person in a comfortable position, lying down, and reassure them while waiting for medical assistance.
