# RCF - Airbnb Guest Interactions (2023) - Security & Police

> Security entries route to building security (+971 000 000 000) for non-urgent matters and to police (999) for urgent or unauthorized-access situations. The Value is locked so the model cannot invent a wrong number.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - suspicious individuals near unit

**Project fit:** Guest reporting unfamiliar people loitering at the rental entrance.

**Value:**

> Immediately follow these steps:
> - Avoid approaching them.
> - Call building security on **+971 000 000 000**.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - late-night noise disturbance

**Project fit:** Guest reporting a loud disturbance in the building at night.

**Value:**

> Follow these steps:
> - Stay in your apartment and keep the door locked.
> - Call the local police department at the emergency number **999** to report the disturbance.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - cameras in common areas

**Project fit:** Guest asking whether the property has surveillance.

**Value:**

> For security reasons, we have cameras in some common areas.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - unauthorised access to unit

**Project fit:** Guest reporting someone has entered the rental unit without permission.

**Value:**

> Immediately follow these steps:
> - Evacuate to a safe area.
> - Call the police immediately on **999** to report the incident.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - emergency contact numbers

**Project fit:** Guest asking who to call for security or police.

**Value:**

> The emergency contact number for building security is **+971 000 000 000**. For immediate security concerns, call the police on **999**.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - personal safety concern

**Project fit:** Guest worried about their or someone else's personal safety.

**Value:**

> Immediately follow these steps:
> - Call the police on **999** and file a report about potential danger to your or someone's safety.
> - Avoid walking alone late at night.
> - Stay aware of your surroundings.
> - Use well-lit and populated routes.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - securing apartment when leaving

**Project fit:** Guest asking how to leave the apartment safely.

**Value:**

> To secure your apartment when leaving:
> - Lock all doors and windows.
> - Don't leave valuables in plain sight.
> - If you suspect unauthorized access upon return, call the local police at **999**.

---

## Context Relevancy: Security - immediate police assistance

**Project fit:** Guest needing urgent police response.

**Value:**

> Call police at the emergency number **999**.
