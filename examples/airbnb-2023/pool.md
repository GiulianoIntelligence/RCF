# RCF - Airbnb Guest Interactions (2023) - Rooftop Pools

> Original V0 use case. Each Context Relevancy is a kind of question a guest might ask. The Value is the exact answer the chatbot returned, written by a human, not generated.

---

## Context Relevancy: Rooftop pool - night access

**Project fit:** Guest asking whether the pool is available outside daytime hours.

**Value:**

> Absolutely, the rooftop infinity pools are accessible 24/7, allowing you to enjoy a nighttime swim with the stunning city lights as your backdrop.

---

## Context Relevancy: Rooftop pool - lifeguard presence

**Project fit:** Guest asking about lifeguard supervision or child safety at the pool.

**Value:**

> There is no lifeguard on duty at the rooftop pools. Please swim responsibly and keep an eye on children.

---

## Context Relevancy: Rooftop pool - depth

**Project fit:** Guest asking how deep the pool is, often to gauge safety or diving suitability.

**Value:**

> The rooftop infinity pools have varying depths, ranging from 1.2 meters (4 feet) to 1.8 meters (6 feet).

---

## Context Relevancy: Rooftop pool - loungers and towels

**Project fit:** Guest asking what amenities are provided poolside.

**Value:**

> Yes, there are comfortable poolside loungers, and fresh towels are provided for your convenience at the rooftop pools.

## Context Relevancy: Rooftop pool - temperature control
